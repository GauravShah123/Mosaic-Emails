Judging Process

Please arrive between 4:45 PM and 5:00 PM and check in at the registration desk. At 5:00 PM, we will hold a short judges‚Äô meeting where our organizers will walk you through the judging criteria and case details. This will ensure you have all the information needed to assess teams fairly. You will also have the opportunity to ask any questions before judging begins.

After the meeting, each judge will be stationed in a separate room with a volunteer. Both judges and participants will receive a schedule indicating when each team will present in their assigned room. The judging will follow a structured format: each team will have 3 minutes to pitch their solution, followed by a 2-minute Q&A session where you can ask clarifying questions.
